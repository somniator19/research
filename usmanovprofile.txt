3.2 Alisher Usmanov

Alisher Usmanov is a Russian-Uzbek businessman and investor, widely known for his holdings in metals, mining, and telecommunications. He is considered a politically exposed person (PEP) due to his significant economic influence and longstanding ties to strategic sectors of the Russian economy.

Usmanov built his wealth through investments in companies such as Metalloinvest and previously held a major stake in the telecommunications company MegaFon. His business activities span key industries, including steel production, mining, and digital infrastructure. These sectors are often regarded as strategically important, contributing to his classification as a high-risk individual within compliance frameworks.

Usmanov has been subject to sanctions by several international jurisdictions. The European Union imposed sanctions in 2022, citing his close links to the Russian political leadership and his role as a prominent business figure benefiting from the system. The United Kingdom also introduced asset freezes and travel restrictions in the same period. Similarly, the United States has applied restrictions through sanctions-related measures targeting individuals connected to the Russian economic and political environment.

These sanctions have included asset freezes, restrictions on financial transactions, and limitations on access to international financial systems. In addition, various luxury assets reportedly linked to Usmanov, such as real estate and yachts, have been targeted under enforcement actions.

From a compliance perspective, Usmanov represents a classic example of an oligarchic PEP, where substantial private wealth intersects with political exposure. Compared to other figures, his case highlights how sanctions frameworks address individuals who may not hold formal political office but are still considered influential within the broader state-linked economic structure.

Overall, the designation of Usmanov across multiple jurisdictions reflects a consistent approach to identifying high-net-worth individuals with potential political connections as elevated risk subjects within AML systems.
