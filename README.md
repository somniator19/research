# 📊 Sanctions Exposure of Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)

This repository contains an independent research project focused on the analysis of **politically exposed persons (PEPs)** and their exposure to international sanctions regimes.

The project combines **open-source intelligence (OSINT)** methods with a structured analytical approach to explore how different categories of individuals are treated within modern **AML (Anti-Money Laundering)** frameworks.

---

## 🧠 Research Objective

The main goal of this research is to:

- Analyze how sanctions are applied to different types of PEPs  
- Compare approaches across major jurisdictions (US, EU, UK)  
- Identify patterns in enforcement mechanisms  
- Demonstrate structured analytical thinking using open data
